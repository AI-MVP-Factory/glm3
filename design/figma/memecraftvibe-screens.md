# MemecraftVibe MVP - Figma Screen Documentation

## Overview
MemecraftVibe is an AI-powered meme creation app that makes it easy to create and share viral content. This document outlines all screens with focus on shareability and viral growth features.

---

## Viral Design Philosophy
- **Shareability:** Easy export and cross-platform sharing
- **Trend Awareness:** Real-time meme templates and formats
- **Expression:** Multiple ways to customize and personalize
- **Community:** Feature users and popular creations
- **Simplicity:** Fast creation with minimal friction
- **Virality:** Built-in sharing hooks and social proof

## Design Specifications

### Color Palette
- Primary: #FF6B6B (Coral) - Eye-catching and energetic
- Secondary: #4ECDC4 (Turquoise) - Fresh and modern
- Accent: #FFE66D (Yellow) - Attention-grabbing
- Success: #95E1D3 (Mint) - Positive reinforcement
- Dark: #2C3E50 (Navy) - For text and contrast
- Background: #F8F9FA (Light) - Clean canvas

### Typography
- Headings: Montserrat, Bold, 32px
- Subheadings: Montserrat, SemiBold, 24px
- Body: Inter, Regular, 16px
- Meme text: Impact, Bold (for templates)
- Labels: Inter, Medium, 14px

### Spacing
- Unit: 8px (base)
- Padding: 16px, 24px, 32px
- Margin: 8px, 16px, 24px
- Component spacing: 20px
- Elevated cards: 8px shadow

### Iconography
- Style: Bold and playful
- Size: 24px (standard), 32px (large)
- Colors: Primary brand, accent for actions
- Custom meme-themed illustrations
- Trending indicators with animations

### Animation Language
- Pop: 200ms cubic-bezier(0.175, 0.885, 0.32, 1.275)
- Smooth: 300ms ease-in-out
- Micro-interactions on all buttons
- Loading states with skeleton screens
- Success celebrations with confetti
- Swipe gestures for template browsing
